Tamper-evident proof that every delivered insight remains exactly as it was emitted — Smart Sentinel AI · XAUUSD M15 · generated 2026-05-23 14:45 UTC
What this dashboard proves. Every InsightSignalV2 we deliver is appended to a SHA-256 hash-chained SQLite ledger. Each entry's hash includes the previous entry's hash, so any post-hoc mutation cascades forward and is caught in O(N) at audit time. The client can re-run verify() at any point — either the chain is intact end-to-end, or it returns the exact seq where the tamper occurred. Note: this is a structural integrity proof — it does not certify the truth of insight bodies, only that the archive matches what was delivered.
